Service About us

Smart Sip offers a range of innovative services designed to enhance hydration habits and promote overall health. One of its primary features is water intake monitoring, which tracks the amount of water consumed throughout the day and allows users to set personalized daily hydration goals based on their individual needs.

To support consistent hydration, Smart Sip provides reminders and notifications, sending alerts to encourage users to drink water at regular intervals, with customizable settings for frequency and timing. The bottle seamlessly integrates with mobile apps via Bluetooth, enabling detailed tracking and analysis of hydration data while syncing with fitness trackers or health applications for a comprehensive view of overall wellness.
